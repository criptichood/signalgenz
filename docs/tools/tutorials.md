# Tutorials & Guides: Your Knowledge Base

The **Tutorials** page is your built-in learning center, designed to help you master both the Signal Gen application and fundamental trading concepts. Whether you're a new user trying to find your way around or an experienced trader looking to refine your knowledge, this section is your go-to resource.

## A Dynamic Learning Experience

To ensure the content is always fresh, relevant, and expanding, all tutorial articles are managed in our database. This modern approach allows us to:
-   **Add New Topics Quickly:** We can add new guides on emerging trading strategies, security practices, or new app features without requiring you to update the application.
-   **Keep Content Current:** Market concepts evolve. A database-driven system means we can update articles instantly to reflect the latest information and best practices.

## How to Use the Tutorials

The interface is designed for easy navigation and learning.

### 1. Topic Navigation

On the left side of the page, you'll find a list of categories. This allows you to quickly jump to the area you're interested in, such as:
-   **How-To Guides:** Step-by-step instructions for using the app's features (e.g., "How to Generate a Signal").
-   **Trading Strategies & Concepts:** Deep dives into technical analysis concepts like Market Structure, Liquidity, and Moving Averages.
-   **Integrations:** Information about the data sources and platforms we connect with, such as exchanges and oracles.
-   **Security:** Essential guides on protecting your assets, from wallet security to avoiding common scams.
-   **Exchanges:** Explanations on the differences between Centralized (CEX) and Decentralized (DEX) exchanges.

### 2. Article Content

When you select a topic, a list of relevant articles appears in the main content area. Each article is presented in an accordion format. Simply click on a title to expand it and read the full content. The articles often include clear, actionable steps and explanations to enhance your understanding.
