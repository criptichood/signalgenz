# Welcome to Signal Gen

Signal Gen is a sophisticated, feature-rich web application designed for cryptocurrency traders. It leverages the power of the Google Gemini API to analyze market data, generate predictive trading signals, and provide a suite of tools for comprehensive market analysis, strategy backtesting, and performance tracking.

This documentation will guide you through the various features and help you get the most out of your AI-powered trading assistant.

## ‚ú® Key Features

- **AI-Powered Signal Generation**: Generate predictive swing and scalp trading signals for various cryptocurrencies using advanced AI models.
- **Natural Language Market Screener**: Use plain English to ask the AI to find assets that match specific technical analysis criteria.
- **Interactive Live Charts**: Real-time candlestick charts with customizable indicators.
- **Advanced Scalping UI**: A dedicated interface for high-frequency trading with specialized tools like an Order Book and Time & Sales.
- **AI Assistant Chat**: A powerful chatbot for contextual help, general trading knowledge, and triggering in-app actions.
- **Trade Simulation & Backtesting**: Test signal performance against historical data or in a live simulated environment.
- **Comprehensive Trade Logging**: Manually log Spot and Perpetual trades to maintain a detailed trading journal.
- **In-Depth Analytics**: A dedicated dashboard to visualize performance metrics like P/L, win rate, and profitability by asset.
- **Social & Community Features**: Build a profile, share posts with attached signals, follow other traders, and engage through comments and direct messages.
- **Strategy Hub**: Create, save, and manage your personal trading strategies.
- **Essential Trading Tools**: A suite of calculators for Position Sizing, Leverage P/L, and Risk/Reward.
