# Dashboard: Your Mission Control

The Dashboard is your central hub, designed to give you a comprehensive, at-a-glance overview of the market and your trading activity. It's the first page you see upon logging in and serves as your launchpad into the application's powerful features.

## Features Overview

### 1. Quick Actions

At the top of the dashboard, you'll find four prominent cards that provide one-click access to the core functions of Signal Gen:

-   **Generate Signal**: Jumps you directly to the **Signal Gen** page for AI-powered swing trade analysis.
-   **Start Scalping**: Takes you to the **Scalping** interface for high-frequency, short-term opportunities.
-   **Run Screener**: Opens the **Screener** page, allowing you to use natural language to find assets matching your criteria.
-   **View Analytics**: Navigates to the **Analytics** dashboard for a deep dive into your trading performance.

### 2. Market Pulse

This section gives you an immediate sense of the current market conditions.

-   **Live BTC Chart**: A real-time candlestick chart for the `BTCUSDT` pair on Binance. It provides a quick reference for the market's overall direction.
-   **AI News Feed**: A curated list of the latest AI-analyzed headlines from top cryptocurrency sources. Click on any headline to read the full article.

### 3. Performance Snapshot & Live Positions

Stay on top of your performance without leaving the dashboard.

-   **Performance Snapshot**: Displays your key performance indicators (KPIs) from your logged perpetuals trades, including your **Total Net P/L** and overall **Win Rate**.
-   **Live Positions**: If you have any active positions on the **Scalping** page, they will be listed here with their symbol, direction, and current unrealized P/L.

### 4. Recent AI Signals

A compact version of the **AI Signal History** table shows your most recently generated signals. You can quickly review their status (Pending, Win, Loss) or click "View All" to navigate to the full history page for more detailed analysis and backtesting.
